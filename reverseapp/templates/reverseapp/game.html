Random game

We often play Interview with friends. The essence of this game is approximately the same, but since we constantly say that “we know everything about everyone”, I suggested making a kind of reverse game.

Rules

  • Take 2 sets of cards according to the number of players. It could also be a number generator, or a bunch of paper names folded into a hat.
  • Write a lot of different personal questions, like “favorite movie” or “who is your crash”. Mix these questions and put them in another hat
  • In turns, take out the name of the person for whom someone will be answering the question and the question that needs to be answered. (If you made two sets of names, then also get a piece of paper with the name of the person who answers).
  • For example, it's my turn to answer (or my name was pulled out of the "who answers" bag). I pull out your name and the question "favorite artist". Then I try to answer that question for you (naming your favorite artist). Rules for answers - fast, but not necessarily short.
